Most tools require an @namontana.org account. This grants you access to the Regional Google Workspace Enterprise, Canva Pro, and our Cloud/AI infrastructure.

  • Use your provided email (e.g., yourname@namontana.org)
  • Set up mandatory 2-Step Verification

About the SM&TS Committee

We are a resource for the entire fellowship. Our mission is to maintain the technical infrastructure that allows addicts to find meetings, events, and recovery literature.

Our Workgroups

Infrastructure Servers, DNS, App Development, & Security.
Website & Content Design, UX, and keeping the meeting list current.
